To My Queen, My Wifey, My Mrs. Sistoso — Inori Senshi Antonio 🌷☺️

Happy Motmot, wifey.

As I look back at our month of June, I realize how much we went through together.

June was not an easy month for us.

There were fears, overthinking, misunderstandings, tears, difficult conversations, and moments where both of us felt mentally and emotionally tired.

There were days when your heart felt heavy because of wounds from the past. Days when fear became louder than peace. Days when overthinking made you question things, even my heart and intentions.

But despite all of that… we’re still here.

Still choosing each other.

And that means so much to me.

This month made me understand your heart even more.

I saw your fears.

I saw your pain.

I saw your overthinking.

I saw the silent battles inside your heart that other people cannot see.

But I also saw something beautiful in you.

Your strength.

Even when things felt heavy, even when your heart was scared, you still kept trying.

You still kept loving me.

You still stayed.

And My Queen, I want you to always remember this.

I still choose you.

Not only during sweet moments.

Not only when everything feels easy.

Not only when there is peace.

I choose you even on difficult days.

I choose you through misunderstandings.

I choose you when fear becomes loud.

I never asked for perfection, wifey.

I never needed a perfect girl.

I just wanted you.

Your heart.

Your softness.

Your faith.

The real you.

And there’s one thing I’ll be selfish with.

Please outlive me ☺️

Because I cannot bear the thought of being alone and living in a world without you.

Because I cannot bear waking up one day and not having you beside me.

But if life turns out differently, and I’m the one who outlive you… know that God knows my heart, and I believe He knows yours too.

So wherever life takes us… wherever eternity takes us… I trust that God will lead our paths back to each other again. 🌷

This June also reminded me that we cannot do this alone.

That’s why I’m grateful that we keep trying to walk with God together.

When fear comes, we pray.

When temptation comes, we rebuke it.

When overthinking comes, we surrender it to Him.

I pray God continues healing every wound inside your heart and removes every fear, insecurity, and pain from your past.

Thank you for staying.

Thank you for loving hubby.

Thank you for choosing us.

No matter what season comes…

I will keep choosing you.

Happy Motmot, My Queen.

Happy Motmot, My Wifey.

Happy Motmot, My Mrs. Sistoso 🌷☺️
